Life Memories

Why wait to start my new life? Some birds are not meant to be caged, their feathers are just too bright.

  • Nov 2001: The Miracle of Birth

    Patient: Is it a boy or a girl?

    Obstetrician: I am a lucky girl in a kind family. This is my treasure and fortune.

  • Sep 2016 to Jul 2019: Studied at No.7 High School

    Happy high school time.

  • Sep 2019: Entered into Renmin University of China (RUC) with major in Statistics

    A new journey with an amazing new subject regarding randomness and probability.

  • Jan 2020: Second failure: Got 64 points in Higher Algebra I Final Exam

    Finally got 80 points--the square root of 64 times 10. Depressed and started to think about transferring to another major.

  • Jan 2020: The Outbreak of Covid-19

    Historical moment. Gained 4 kilograms because of laziness and eating (the maximum weight is 68 kg). Depressed because of lockdown. Realized that life is so transient.

  • May 2020 to Aug 2020: Lose Weight by Punction Therapy And Use OK Glass to Cure Eyesight

    Lost 12 kilograms. Had 5.2 eyesight during daytime without wearing glasses. Feeling the world is much better.

  • May 2021 to Aug 2021: Research At LISA Lab Hosted by Prof. Wei Xu at RUC

    Handled my first research project independently. Much effort was paid into it. My research interest changed to Information Systems.

  • Jul 2021 to Aug 2021: Summer School

    Attended summer school held by RUC and Statistical Center, Peking University. Great thanks to Prof. Weijie Su and Prof. Yuling Jiao's introduction to statistical learning and high dimensional statistics. They are very active and professional researchers! I started to think about going further in statistics.

  • Sep 2021 to Jan 2022: Exchange Student at the University of Hong Kong

    Became a research assistant for Prof. Tuan and Prof. Ivy Dang . Became a research assistant for Prof. Dan Yang and Prof. Haipeng Shen . The first group focuses on quantitative marketing and information systems. The second group focuses on genes and statistics. They want to solve statistical genomics problems with a unique dataset from a hospital. Although this project failed, my research interest changed to statistical genomics/biostatistics.

  • Dec 2021 - June 2022: Undergraduate Research Program at RUC

    I am guided by my class instructor Prof. Yifan Sun . She is patient. We focus on subgroup analysis and want to propose a more general algorithm under fewer assumptions.

  • Feb 2022 - present: Started my first research project and my graduation thesis at UC Berkeley

    I am exteremely fortunate to be guided by Prof. Jingshen Wang at Division of Biostatistics, UC Berkeley. She is an excellent advisor. I learned to be as rigorous as her in research.

  • May 2022 - Sep 2022: Research Intern at Harvard University

    So lucky to have opportunity to study at Prof. Jun S. Liu 's lab at Department of Statistics, Harvard Univeristy. I became interested in Computational Biology and Causal Inference. Jun is very talented and kind. I also met good alumni Xiaodong and friends Beining and Yuanchuan from USTC. They are gifted rising stars in statistics and helped me much in my application school list and application.

  • Jul 2022 - present: Research Intern at UC Los Angeles

    I began working on bioinformatics and statistical genomics at Prof. Jingyi Jessica Li's lab JSB at Department of Statistics, UC Los Angeles and am advised by Dr. Xinzhou Ge . I also worked together with Zhengtong from UCLA on the project. This project opens a door for me to Bioinformatics.

  • Oct 22, 2022 - Feb 22, 2023: Applying to PhD Programs in the United States and Application Results

    Take much time, effort, and patience. It is difficult to start something new. Just be patient and self-motivated. The best is yet to come. I got offers from Biostatistics MS Thesis program at University of Washington, Seattle , Biostatistics MA/PhD program at UC Berkeley , Biostatistics PhD program at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor , and Statistics PhD program at Rutgers University . Feeling so honored and surprised. I finally accepted an offer from UC Berkeley.

  • June 30, 2023: Graduation from Renmin University of China

    I obtained a Bachelor of Science degree as a major and a Bachelor of Finance as a minor. I won the outstanding thesis at Renmin University of China.

  • Aug 21, 2023: New Journey at UC Berkeley

    Berkeley is a good place to study and have fun. The biggest thing I learned in Berkeley is that rejection is the regular academic life. Do not get frustrated in the true life.

  • Dec 24, 2024: The Biggest Gift from the World So Far

    I am extremely fortunate and appreciated to have my best boyfriend in the world. Thanks so much for accepting my love confession.

  • Aug 2023 - May 2025 MA Journey at UC Berkeley

    I developed my interest in Alzheimer's disease. Especially in AI tools usage for AD. Also, I am exploring my interest in learning theory and causal inference.

  • Future: Choice of Career

    Academia or Industry? I vote for academia now. But I am open to all opportunities and challenges.
